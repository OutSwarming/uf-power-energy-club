Why PEC

Power is everywhere.
Your path into it starts here.

We make power and energy engineering approachable—whether you already speak in megawatts or are just wondering what keeps the lights on.

Through technical workshops, speaker events, projects, plant tours, and career development, members turn classroom concepts into practical skills and industry connections.

Featured workshop

Power flow,
from diagram to decision.

In our Intro to PowerWorld workshop, members trace a nine-bus system, solve power flow, inspect voltage and line loading, then change demand to watch the network react.

  • 01 Model buses, generators, lines, and loads
  • 02 Run steady-state power flow analysis
  • 03 Test real operating changes and constraints
